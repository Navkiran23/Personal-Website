Travel Diaries

Vacations

India

My family and I go to india every year (covid stopped that). India is so full of culture and the food there is just so delicious. We usually spend a month there doing so many things like shopping in Jalandhar, going to the malls in Chandigarh and much more!

Hawaii

I went to Hawaii August of 2021. Hawaii is a very calming place to go vacation. I went to kahului and Maui. My family and I went to a volcano, along with an aquarium and more! It was one of the most beautiful places I have been to. However one wierd thing was that there were roosters everywhere.
